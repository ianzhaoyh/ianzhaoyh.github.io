About Me

Hi there! I'm Yunhao Zhao — you can call me Ian.

I just finished my B.S. in Computer Science at Macau University of Science and Technology, and I'll be joining Northwestern University this Fall 2026 for an M.S. in Computer Science.

My research interests sit at the intersection of embodied intelligence and generative modeling — broadly around VLA, World Models, Reinforcement Learning, and Video Generation.

I believe the next leap in AI will come from machines that learn not just from data, but from doing. My goal is to contribute to that future.
